What we do

Three pillars of impact

We work at the intersection of technology, environment, and community to create lasting change for underrepresented youth and immigrant families.

💻

Technology education

Digital skills and tech literacy programs for African and Black youth and immigrant families in Portland.

☀️

Climate action

♻️

Waste reduction

Composting, recycling, and circular economy practices that protect our natural resources and communities.

Our mission

Tech-driven environmental justice

To advance tech-driven environmental justice by equipping underrepresented youth and immigrant families with digital, environmental, and leadership skills that promote climate resilience, clean energy awareness, and community well-being.

  • Educate and empower youth to become climate-conscious innovators and environmental advocates
  • Reduce waste and promote circular economy practices such as composting, recycling, and reusing
  • Foster community-driven solutions that protect natural resources while addressing social and economic impacts of climate change
  • Build bridges between technology and sustainability to create long-term, inclusive environmental impact
